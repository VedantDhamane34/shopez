# Roles and Responsibilities

The ShopEZ platform involves multiple roles that interact with the system. Each role has specific permissions and responsibilities within the platform.

## Customer (User)

Customers are the primary users of the platform who interact with the product catalog and make purchases.

Responsibilities:
- Register and manage their account
- Browse available products
- View product details and reviews
- Add products to the shopping cart
- Place orders through the checkout system
- Provide product reviews and ratings
- View order history and purchase records

## Administrator (Admin)

Administrators are responsible for managing the platform and ensuring smooth operation of the system.

Responsibilities:
- Add, update, or delete products
- Manage product categories
- Monitor and manage customer orders
- Maintain product inventory
- Review and moderate customer feedback
- Monitor platform activity and ensure system reliability

## System

The system itself is responsible for processing requests, managing data, and maintaining secure communication between components.

Responsibilities:
- Authenticate users securely
- Process product and order data
- Maintain database records
- Handle API requests between frontend and backend
- Ensure secure transactions and data integrity
